Quick Answers

Frequently Asked

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 40 countries. Standard international delivery takes 7–14 business days. Free shipping on orders over £60.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact us to initiate a return.

Can I get a book recommendation?

Absolutely! Tell us what genres or books you've loved, and our team will put together a personalised recommendation list just for you.
