Our Story

A small shop born out of a big love for books and beautiful things.

How It Began

A Corner Shop Turned Into a Community

The Ink & Page Shop started as a single bookshelf in a tiny Notting Hill flat. Founders Clara and Thomas — a librarian and a graphic designer — had one shared obsession: the feeling of holding a beautifully made book or the satisfying scratch of a fine pen across crisp paper.

What began as weekend market stalls became a beloved local shop, then an online home for readers and writers around the world. Every item we stock has been personally chosen — we don't carry anything we wouldn't keep on our own shelves.

Today, thousands of book lovers call The Ink & Page their favourite shop. But the heart of it remains the same: curation, care, and a deep belief that a good book can change everything.

Our Journey

Milestones

2018
The First Stall

Clara and Thomas set up their first weekend market stall at Portobello Road with 60 books and 20 stationery items.

2019
A Permanent Home

Opened our first physical shop at 42 Bookbinder Lane, Notting Hill. Sold out in the first weekend.

2021
Going Online

Launched inkandpageshop.com during lockdown — reaching readers across 30+ countries within the first month.

2023
10,000 Customers

Celebrated our 10,000th order with a special edition gift box sent to a lucky customer in Toronto.

2025
More Than a Shop

Launched our reading clubs, author events, and creative workshops. The Ink & Page is now a full community.

What We Stand For

Our Mission & Values

We believe that beautiful, thoughtfully made things have the power to inspire — and that books are the most powerful things of all.

Curation

Every book and stationery item is hand-selected. We read, test, and handle each product before it earns a place in our shop. No filler, only favourites.

Community

We host reading clubs, workshops, and author events — because great books are even better when shared. Our community is at the heart of everything we do.

Quality

We believe in things that last — books that age gracefully, notebooks that feel wonderful, pens that don't let you down. No compromise on quality.
